## STEP 4: CREATE YOUR INTAKE FORM

Go to: **Settings → Forms & Questionnaires → + Create Form**

**Form Name:** NeuroRefinement — New Client Intake
**Auto-send:** Yes — trigger on first booking
**Required before session:** Yes

---

### Section 1: Contact Information
(Practice Better has built-in fields for most of these — use their defaults)
- Full Name
- Email
- Phone
- Date of Birth
- Emergency Contact Name & Phone

### Section 2: What Brings You Here?
**Field type: Paragraph text**
**Label:** What brings you to NeuroRefinement? What are you hoping to achieve?
**Required:** Yes

### Section 3: Areas of Concern
**Field type: Checkboxes (select all that apply)**
**Label:** Which areas of your body are you most concerned about?
**Options:**
- Neck / Upper back
- Shoulders / Arms / Hands
- Lower back / Hips
- Legs / Knees / Feet
- Balance / Coordination
- Whole body tension or fatigue
- Posture / Desk-related discomfort
- Athletic performance
- Other (please describe)

### Section 4: Pain & Comfort Level
**Field type: Scale (1-10)**
**Label:** On a typical day, how would you rate your overall comfort level?
(1 = significant daily pain, 10 = pain-free)

### Section 5: Movement History
**Field type: Checkboxes (select all that apply)**
**Label:** Have you tried any of the following?
**Options:**
- Physical therapy
- Chiropractic
- Massage therapy
- Yoga or Pilates
- Feldenkrais Method
- Anat Baniel Method / NeuroMovement
- Personal training
- Meditation / Mindfulness
- Acupuncture
- None of the above

### Section 6: Your Goals
**Field type: Checkboxes (select all that apply)**
**Label:** What are your primary goals? (Select all that apply)
**Options:**
- Reduce pain or discomfort
- Move with more ease and freedom
- Improve balance or coordination
- Recover from injury or surgery
- Improve posture
- Enhance athletic performance
- Reduce stress or tension
- Explore personal growth / strengths
- Support a child's development
- Other (please describe)

### Section 7: Current Health
**Field type: Paragraph text**
**Label:** Please list any current diagnoses, injuries, surgeries,
or health conditions we should be aware of.
**Required:** Yes

### Section 8: Stress & Energy
**Field type: Scale (1-10)**
**Label:** How would you rate your current stress level?
(1 = very low stress, 10 = extremely stressed)

**Field type: Scale (1-10)**
**Label:** How would you rate your typical energy level?
(1 = exhausted most of the time, 10 = consistently energized)

### Section 9: How Did You Hear About Us?
**Field type: Dropdown**
**Options:**
- Google search
- Friend or family referral
- Healthcare provider referral
- Social media
- Church / Community group
- Other

### Section 10: Informed Consent & Scope of Practice
**Field type: Consent checkbox (required)**
**Text:**

I understand that NeuroRefinement provides educational wellness
services — not medical treatment, diagnosis, or therapy. Sessions
and lessons are designed to help me explore movement, awareness,
and function through neuroplasticity-based learning. They are not
a substitute for medical care.

I understand that Bryan Thunstrom is a certified Anat Baniel
Method® NeuroMovement® practitioner and strengths-based coach,
not a licensed medical professional, physical therapist, or
psychologist.

I agree to consult my healthcare provider before beginning if I
have a medical condition, recent injury, or am pregnant. I will
communicate openly about my comfort during sessions and stop any
movement that doesn't feel right.

I consent to receive services from NeuroRefinement and understand
I may discontinue at any time.

**Signature field:** Yes (digital signature)
**Date field:** Auto-populate
